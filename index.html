📋 Cruise Reminders & Sky Class Tips
Policies and tips for the Allure sailing — tailored for Sky Class guests.
🥂 Alcohol Policy Each adult may bring one 750ml bottle of wine or champagne on boarding day via carry-on. No boxed wine. All other purchased bottles stored until final day.
🧴 Reef-Safe Sunscreen Required Jamaica & Bahamas ban oxybenzone, octinoxate, octocrylene & titanium dioxide. Buy reef-safe before you go — hard to find at ports.
📱 Online Check-In Complete at RoyalCaribbean.com by June 18 (3 days before sailing). Print SetSail passes and bring them to the pier.
💊 Medications in Carry-On Keep all medications in carry-on, in original labeled containers. Pack 3 extra days in case of itinerary changes.
🎭 Shows: Use Your Concierge Broadway productions and AquaTheater shows have reserved Sky Class seating sections. Share your show preferences in the suite welcome email — no need to book independently beforehand.
👑 Sky Class Boarding As Sky Class guests, you board through a dedicated priority lane before general boarding. No need to race to the pier — you won't wait in the main queue.
🍳 Skip the Buffet for Breakfast Room service breakfast is included in your suite. Or head to Coastal Kitchen — the suite-exclusive restaurant with sit-down service. Both beat the Windjammer every time.
💵 $1 Bills for the Ports Jamaica and Nassau have a strong tip culture. Bring a stack of $1 bills for vendors, local guides, and photo opportunities ashore.
